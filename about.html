    Why WOW

    WOW Payments was founded in 2016 to blaze the trail for a new kind of merchant service provider, one that was not afraid to step outside of the box and deliver the services and solutions that merchants, and sales partners needed.

    With the ability to compete with much larger providers while retaining that smaller, boutique ISO partner experience, WOW began to change how merchants were handled.

    With years of experience working in the merchant services industry, we realized something was seriously wrong. Merchant service providers were cheating business owners out of their hard-earned profits by tacking on huge markups and hidden fees.

    Our goal is to save businesses from greedy payment processors by eliminating those costly markups and hidden fees. Additionally, to help business owners grow, retain, and make money by using the latest technologies available.

    4,000% + increase in activations and processed volume

    This shift in card acceptance philosophy led to a 4,000% + increase in activations and processed volume over the course of 4 years.

    We were recognized by Entrepenuer360 for our accomplishments in 2016, 2018, and 2019 as well as made the INC 500/5000 list for 2018, 2019, and 2020.

    Through our strategic partnerships with Processors, Sponsor Banks, and Reseller Partners, we continue to grow and provide our merchants with options to better serve their businesses and communities.

    Now fast forward to 2023 and the new WOW

    WOW is not just a payments company focusing on retail business. We have expanded our offerings and services through strategic partnerships.

    We will now have the ability to partner with technology and software companies along with venturing into new and developing verticals. Phase one which included going to market with a unique and proprietary terminal offering has already been completed and is allowing us to segway and compete with other well know hardware providers.

    Get Started

    The next part of this venture will be the release of our digital banking solution called WOW Banking Services. Having the ability to approve a merchant for merchant processing and a business checking account will streamline the entire activation process and give the merchant the control over their funding as they need.

    Let's Partner

    WOW, and its partners are primed for success and ready to provide our merchants and their customers with the level of service and offerings to be expected from a leader in the industry.

    Funds will be available for immediate use and through the usage of the WOW Bank Card, they will open themselves up to exciting and valuable rewards. WOW now has the footprint of a smaller niche ISO partner but with the infrastructure and technology of any major competitor in the space. We are built efficiently and are capable of getting creative and flexible when needed – there is no problem or opportunity too small or too large.

    We are not afraid to be different!
